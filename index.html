02 - About

A bit about me.

I'm a design-minded software engineer who's spent three years shipping products people actually interact with. I'm most at home at the intersection of design thinking and technical execution.

What drives me is making complex things feel simple and intuitive. Whether it's a casino game that needs to communicate its rules in seconds, or a stealth AI that needs to feel fair and readable. I care about how things feel.

At Logifuture, I championed the transition to AI-driven development, ran educational sessions for my team, and built a shared library of Claude skills. I see AI as a tool that lets me stay focused on architecture and the bigger picture.
