01 - The Original Mini Game

A quick bet became a million-bet initiative

Screenshot placeholder

Designed and pitched a mini casino game embedded in the sportsbook bet confirmation flow. A fast follow-on wager that felt natural, not intrusive. It delivered 170k bets in launch week and became the template for a wider mini-games initiative used in new-client pitches.

Later pushed a UX improvement that increased average daily bets from 10k to 60k. A 6x lift from a single interaction change.
