Logifuture

Casino Games

At Logifuture I've worked on 8+ game releases from start to finish and helped maintain their portfolio of 40+ games and contributed to the overall game platform and team tools. All the games are released on Bet9ja.com one of Nigeria's highest traffic websites. I also redesigned, pitched and implemented a mini version of their flagship game to drive user engagement from sportsbook, the far more popular side of Bet9ja to casino product. After a strong initial performance I rebuilt 2 more games for the initiative which have now acchieved over a million bets across the 3 games.

Role

Game Developer

Timeline

2023 – Present

Stack

01 - Spin & Win+

Spin & Win+

Spin & Win+ is Logifuture's crowning game and the second most played game on the whole website. It was created as a Pixi version of the website's long time most popular game. On Spin & Win+ I was trusted with the ownership of the zoom in, free spin and small win features. The zoom in is essential, it serves to increase the tension of the game and make wins OR losses feel exciting. For the free spins I opted to have the background transition between all the colours already used on the wheel and increased the rate and intesity of the background star blinking feature. As the name implies players are getting another entire bet for free, so it was important to really let the user enjoy the moment.
